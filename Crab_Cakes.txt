Crab Cakes:

Ingredients:
(1/2) cup garlic mashes potatoes
1 Lb crab meat - refrigerated
(1/4) cup Panko Bread Crumbs
1-2 egg yolks
(3/4) of a red pepper diced fine

Directions:
Form cakes, dust in flour, dip in egg wash (egg beaten with water), dip in Panko.
Chill for several hours.
Saute on low in olive oil until heated through.

Serve on sour-dough bread with mayonnaise and fresh lemon.
