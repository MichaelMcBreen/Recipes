Honey White Bread
1 1/3 Cup skim milk
2 tablespoons Olive Oil
3 Tablespoons honey
3 ¾ Bread flour
1 Tablespoon Vital Wheat Gluten – Optional
1 ½ Teaspoon salt
1 packet instant yeast – (2 ¼ teaspoons)

Mix all ingredients together.  Kneed for about 15 minutes, until smooth & elastic.
Put in greased bowl, cover, let rise until doubles.
Punch down, form  into loaf, put in greased loaf pan, let rise for (1/2) hour.
Bake in 350 degree oven until done, about 35 minutes.
